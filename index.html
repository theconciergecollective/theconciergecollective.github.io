Services curated around your recovery

         
Non‑medical, highly coordinated
       
       
         

            We operate as your behind‑the‑scenes partner—designing a recovery experience that feels intentional, supported, and discreet. While your surgeon and clinical team focus on outcomes, we focus on everything that surrounds them: environment, comfort, timing, and the licensed professionals you choose to invite into your space.          

         
           
             
Pre‑operative
             

Pre‑op planning & arrival support

             

                We help you think through the details before surgery: arrival timelines, proximity to your surgeon, recovery‑friendly accommodations, and the rhythm of your first 72 hours post‑op. We coordinate logistics so your day‑of feels calm, not chaotic.              

             

                Examples: transportation coordination, check‑in timing, recovery‑ready groceries, light household support.              

           
           
             
Post‑operative
             

Recovery environment & daily rhythm

             

                From quiet, prepared spaces to thoughtfully timed visits, we help shape a recovery environment that supports rest and privacy. We coordinate schedules, arrivals, and access so you’re never managing a revolving door of people when you should be resting.              

             

                Examples: arrival windows, light meal coordination, discreet visitor management, comfort‑focused touches.              

           
           
             
After‑care coordination
             

Licensed nurses & therapeutic support

             

                When desired, we coordinate with licensed third‑party professionals—such as private duty nurses, lymphatic drainage massage therapists, and other vetted recovery providers—to support your healing in alignment with your surgeon’s guidance.              

             

                We do not provide medical care or advice. All clinical services are delivered solely by independent, licensed professionals.

We clarify your goals, boundaries, and the level of support you’d like around your surgeon’s plan—always respecting that your medical decisions remain between you and your clinical team.

After‑care support orchestration
               

                  If you choose, we coordinate with licensed third‑party professionals—such as private duty nurses, lymphatic massage therapists, or other recovery specialists—to align schedules, access, and expectations in support of your surgeon’s post‑op guidance.                

             
           
           
             
4
             
               
Recovery rhythm & refinement
               

                  As you move through recovery, we remain your point of contact for adjustments to logistics, timing, and access. We do not direct clinical care, but we help ensure your support ecosystem remains organized, discreet, and aligned with your needs.                

             
           
         
         
           
What we are — and are not
           

              We are a non‑medical concierge focused on orchestration, not intervention. We do not replace your surgeon, nursing team, or any licensed provider. Instead, we sit beside the clinical plan—supporting the logistics, environment, and people around it.            

           

              Any nursing, therapeutic, or wellness services are delivered exclusively by independent, licensed third‑party professionals. We coordinate and schedule; they provide care within their own scope of practice.            

           

              This distinction matters—to your safety, to your outcomes, and to the integrity of everyone involved in your recovery.
